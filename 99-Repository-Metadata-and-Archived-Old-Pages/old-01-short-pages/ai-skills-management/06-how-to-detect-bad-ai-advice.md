# How To Detect Bad AI Advice

## What Problem This Solves

Scholars see many viral AI recommendations that are not designed for research quality, confidentiality, or reproducibility.

## Bad Advice Often Says

- use these 20 tools
- this prompt writes your paper
- replace your RA with AI
- automate your literature review fully
- generate a publishable paper in three hours
- no need to learn coding anymore
- no need to use Git

## Good Advice Shows

- workflow
- constraints
- risks
- verification
- examples
- tool versions or last-checked date
- what can go wrong

## Test Before Adopting

Try advice on a small, nonconfidential research task. Keep it only if it improves accuracy, traceability, or time use without increasing hidden risk.

## Sources and Workflow Influences

Draws on builder-oriented AI learning, academic workflow guides, and responsible research practice.
