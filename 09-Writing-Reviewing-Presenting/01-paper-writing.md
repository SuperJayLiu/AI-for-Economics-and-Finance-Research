# Paper Writing

AI can help improve the clarity and structure of economics and finance papers, but it should not invent the paper's contribution.

Use AI to test whether the draft clearly answers:

- What is the economic question?
- Why does it matter?
- What is the mechanism?
- What is the identification strategy or theoretical result?
- What is the main finding?
- What is the economic magnitude?
- How does it relate to existing literature?

Verify that AI edits preserve meaning, evidence, citations, and hedging.
