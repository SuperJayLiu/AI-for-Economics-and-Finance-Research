# How To Evaluate A Slide Skill

## What Problem This Solves

Slide-generation skills can produce attractive decks while weakening the research. Evaluate the workflow, not only the visual output.

## A Good Slide Skill Includes

- clear workflow phases
- required inputs
- privacy warnings
- visual preview before final generation
- accessible and responsive output
- content overflow handling
- export to PDF
- sharing or deployment option
- ability to preserve images/assets from PPT
- clear license
- clear installation instructions
- verification checklist for claims, numbers, figures, and labels

## Economics And Finance Checks

- Does it preserve table notes and figure labels?
- Does it avoid changing causal claims?
- Does it support equations and notation?
- Does it distinguish empirical charts from illustrative diagrams?
- Does it prevent over-animated slides from distracting from identification?
- Does it support backup slides for robustness and Q&A?
