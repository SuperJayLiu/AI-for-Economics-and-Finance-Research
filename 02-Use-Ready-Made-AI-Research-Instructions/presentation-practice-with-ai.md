# Practice Your Research Presentation With AI

## Purpose

Use AI as a practice audience for an economics or finance presentation.

## Required Inputs

- talk title
- target audience
- talk length
- abstract or slide outline
- main identification/model/result
- likely weak points
- what kind of audience to simulate: friendly seminar, tough finance audience, applied micro audience, theory audience, policy audience

## Workflow

1. Ask AI to summarize the talk's core claim in one sentence.
2. Ask for unclear parts of the story.
3. Ask for 10 likely audience questions.
4. Ask for 5 hostile but fair questions.
5. Draft answers yourself.
6. Ask AI to critique whether answers overclaim.
7. Revise slides or backup slides.
8. Practice a timed version.

## Prompt Starter

```text
Act as a tough but fair economics/finance seminar audience.
My talk is about [topic].
Audience: [audience].
Talk length: [minutes].
Core claim: [claim].
Identification/model: [design].
Main results: [results].

Ask questions that would reveal unclear motivation, weak identification, missing mechanism, overstated contribution, or confusing figures. Do not invent facts. Flag which questions require backup slides.
```

## Verification Checklist

- Do answers match actual results?
- Do answers avoid unsupported causal claims?
- Are backup slides based on completed analysis?
- Are speculative answers clearly marked as speculation?
