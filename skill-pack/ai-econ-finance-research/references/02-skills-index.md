## Universal Skill Behavior

Every skill should:

1. collect missing inputs;
2. ask clarifying questions if needed;
3. confirm structure before long output;
4. produce a usable artifact;
5. list verification steps;
6. state what was produced and what remains uncertain.

## Do Not Use Placeholder Fields

Do not ask the user to fill blanks mechanically. Ask questions in plain language instead.

Example:

```text
Tell me your research question, possible data source, possible method, and target audience. If you are unsure about any of these, say "unknown" and I will help you clarify.
```
