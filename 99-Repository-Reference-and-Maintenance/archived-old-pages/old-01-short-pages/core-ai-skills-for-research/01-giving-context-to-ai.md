# Giving Context To AI

Good context helps AI produce useful output and helps you detect errors.

Provide:

- your research question
- field and subfield
- stage of the project
- intended audience
- source materials
- definitions of key variables or models
- output format
- verification requirements

Do not provide confidential or restricted material unless the tool and policy permit it.

Ask the AI to state assumptions, uncertainty, and what it did not check.
