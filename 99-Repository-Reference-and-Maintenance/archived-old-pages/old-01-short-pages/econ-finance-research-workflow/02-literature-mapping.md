# Literature Mapping

Literature mapping is more than summarizing papers. It identifies how research questions, mechanisms, methods, evidence, and debates relate to each other.

AI can help by:

- organizing verified papers by theme
- comparing mechanisms
- separating data, model, identification, and contribution
- finding possible missing literatures
- drafting synthesis structures

The scholar must verify:

- every source
- every claim about contribution
- every claim about identification or theory
- whether the map overstates novelty

Use AI to make the map easier to inspect, not to decide the literature for you.
