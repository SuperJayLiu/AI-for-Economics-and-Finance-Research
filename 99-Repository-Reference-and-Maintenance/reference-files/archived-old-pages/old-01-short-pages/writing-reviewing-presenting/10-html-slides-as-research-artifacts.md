# HTML Slides As Research Artifacts

## What Problem This Solves

HTML slides are useful when you want a presentation that is easy to share, version-control, customize, publish online, or export to PDF.

## When HTML Slides Are Good

- conference talks
- seminar websites
- online teaching
- research project pages
- reproducible workshop materials
- public-facing research explainers
- interactive visual explanations

## When PowerPoint Or Keynote May Be Better

- strict conference templates
- coauthors who only edit PPT
- institutional branding requirements
- offline rooms with uncertain browser support
- talks requiring quick last-minute edits by nontechnical collaborators

## Economics And Finance Style Rule

Interactive slides should clarify mechanism, identification, data, and results. They should not turn a research talk into a product demo.

## Sources And Workflow Influences

Draws on zero-dependency HTML presentation skills, visual preview workflows, PPT-to-web conversion, PDF export workflows, and anti-generic design practices.
