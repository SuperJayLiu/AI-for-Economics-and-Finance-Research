# Submission Checks

Before submitting a paper, presentation, replication package, or referee response that used AI assistance, check:

- journal or conference AI policy
- coauthor agreement
- disclosure statement
- citation verification
- data-use compliance
- code review and reproducibility
- figure and table provenance
- whether AI-generated wording changed substantive meaning
- whether confidential material was processed appropriately

Keep an internal AI-use log even when disclosure is not required.
