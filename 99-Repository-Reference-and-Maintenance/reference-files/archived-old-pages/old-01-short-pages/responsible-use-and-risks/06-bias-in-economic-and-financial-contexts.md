# Bias In Economic And Financial Contexts

AI systems can reproduce biases in training data, institutional coverage, language, geography, and publication visibility.

Research risks include:

- overrepresenting highly cited US or English-language work
- underrepresenting regional institutions or non-English sources
- overstating market efficiency or policy effects
- giving generic causal interpretations
- missing distributional consequences
- simplifying institutional constraints

When using AI for critique, ask it to identify assumptions, missing populations, external-validity limits, and alternative mechanisms.
