# Personal Academic Website Template

## Purpose

Create a simple academic website for an economics or finance scholar using GitHub Pages or Google Sites.

## Best For

- PhD students
- job market candidates
- junior faculty
- researchers who need a clean public profile
- scholars who want a stable page for papers, slides, code, and teaching

## Option A: GitHub Pages

Best when you want version control, Markdown, and a durable research page.

Required inputs:

- name
- title and affiliation
- research fields
- short bio
- working papers
- publications
- CV PDF
- contact information
- Google Scholar/SSRN/ORCID/GitHub links
- teaching
- talks and slides

Suggested structure:

```text
index.md
papers.md
teaching.md
talks.md
cv.pdf
assets/
```

## Option B: Google Sites

Best when you want fast editing, no Git setup, and easy visual layout.

Required inputs are the same, but keep source PDFs and paper metadata backed up outside Google Sites.

## AI Workflow

1. Draft a plain academic bio.
2. Ask AI to shorten it for homepage use.
3. Ask AI to organize papers by status.
4. Ask AI to check for inflated claims.
5. Verify every link.
6. Publish only public materials.

## Verification Checklist

- Are paper titles correct?
- Are coauthor names correct?
- Are links public and stable?
- Are unpublished materials approved for sharing?
- Is the CV current?
- Are claims about grants, awards, and placements accurate?
