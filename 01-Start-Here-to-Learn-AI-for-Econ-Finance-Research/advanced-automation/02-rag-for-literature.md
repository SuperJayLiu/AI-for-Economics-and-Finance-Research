# RAG For Literature

RAG can help query a curated literature library, but it does not remove the need to read and verify sources.

Good uses:

- finding passages in uploaded papers
- comparing concepts across a known source set
- building source-grounded notes
- locating where a claim may be discussed

Risks:

- retrieving irrelevant passages
- missing important sources
- overstating what a passage says
- confusing source text with interpretation

Require citations to specific source locations and check them manually.
