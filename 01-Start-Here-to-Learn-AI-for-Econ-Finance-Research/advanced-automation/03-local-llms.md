# Local LLMs

Local LLMs can help when privacy or control matters, but they are not automatically accurate or compliant.

Potential benefits:

- data stays on local or institution-controlled hardware
- workflows can be customized
- sensitive files may be handled with lower external exposure

Limits:

- setup burden
- weaker performance in some tasks
- hallucination still occurs
- policy approval may still be needed

Use local models as part of a broader data-safety plan.
