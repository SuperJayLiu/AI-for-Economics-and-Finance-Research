# Finance-Specific Risks

## What Problem This Solves

Finance research is especially exposed to AI-amplified factor mining, narrative generation, and backtest overfitting.

## Risks

- factor zoo expansion
- p-hacking and HARKing
- backtest overfitting
- look-ahead bias
- survivorship bias
- transaction cost omission
- data leakage
- LLM sentiment trading claims
- investment advice overreach

## Core Message

AI can make it easy to generate many plausible finance papers. That makes research taste, theory, data quality, identification, and honesty more important, not less.

## What The Scholar Must Verify

- out-of-sample evidence
- data timing
- transaction costs
- benchmark construction
- multiple-testing correction
- economic mechanism
- whether the finding is a narrative after the fact

## Sources and Workflow Influences

Draws on finance research integrity concerns and AI-powered finance scholarship discussions.
