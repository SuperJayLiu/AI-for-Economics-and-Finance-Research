# Confidential Data And Referee Reports

Do not upload confidential referee reports, manuscripts, grant proposals, or review materials unless the relevant policy explicitly permits the AI tool and its privacy setting.

Do not upload restricted or confidential data to public AI systems unless the data-use agreement and institutional policy allow it.

High-risk materials include:

- referee reports
- confidential manuscripts
- restricted administrative data
- transaction-level financial data
- proprietary firm data
- student records
- private seminar comments
- coauthor drafts without permission

Use institution-approved tools, local processing, or manually written summaries when confidentiality is uncertain.
