# Literature Review Project

## Purpose

Help a researcher organize, compare, and critique papers in economics or finance.

## Best For

- PhD field papers
- related work sections
- new project positioning
- grant proposals
- referee responses

## Not Suitable For

- inventing literature
- replacing reading
- deciding novelty without expert review
- citing papers that have not been checked

## What To Upload

- key papers you are allowed to upload
- your research question
- existing notes
- target journal, seminar, or field audience
- bibliography file if available

## What Not To Upload

- confidential manuscripts without permission
- restricted seminar materials
- copyrighted files where upload is not allowed
- private referee reports unless policy permits use

## Project Instructions

- Prioritize accuracy over fluency.
- Never invent citations.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Distinguish model, data, identification, results, limitations, and contribution.
- Flag uncertain claims.
- Identify what must be checked manually.
- Do not claim a gap in the literature unless the source base supports it.

## Recommended Workflow

1. Start with a source inventory.
2. Create structured notes for each paper.
3. Compare papers by question, mechanism, data, method, and finding.
4. Group papers by debate or mechanism, not only by chronology.
5. Ask for missing literatures or alternative interpretations.
6. Verify all citations and claims.
7. Draft a synthesis only after verification.

## What The AI Should Produce

- paper comparison tables
- thematic maps
- lists of uncertainties
- possible connections to your project
- draft synthesis paragraphs with verification notes

## What The Scholar Must Verify

- every citation
- every claim about a paper's contribution
- every claim about identification, model, or dataset
- whether the literature map is incomplete
- whether the AI overstated novelty

## Common Mistakes

- asking for a broad literature review with no source base
- accepting invented references
- confusing paper summary with synthesis
- treating a polished paragraph as evidence of coverage

## Disclosure Notes

Record whether AI assisted with source organization, synthesis, or language editing.
