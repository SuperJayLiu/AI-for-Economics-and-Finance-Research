# What Are AI Projects?

In this handbook, an AI project means a reusable workspace for a specific research job, with clear purpose, uploaded materials, operating rules, verification steps, and expected outputs.

AI projects are useful because research tasks are rarely one-off prompts. A literature review, empirical paper, theory model, replication package, or seminar talk has repeated context and recurring standards.

## Project Components

- purpose
- best use cases
- materials to upload
- materials not to upload
- project instructions
- workflow
- expected outputs
- scholar verification duties
- disclosure and privacy notes

## Important Note

Product features change. Before relying on a tool's project, file, memory, or privacy behavior, check the current official documentation and your institution's policy.
