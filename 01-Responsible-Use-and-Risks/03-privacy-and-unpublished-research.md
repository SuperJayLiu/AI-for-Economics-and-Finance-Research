# Privacy And Unpublished Research

Unpublished research can contain valuable ideas, sensitive data, confidential seminar feedback, or proprietary information.

Before uploading material to an AI system, check:

- tool privacy settings
- institutional policy
- coauthor consent
- data-use agreements
- journal or conference rules
- whether content may be used for model training
- whether the provider stores or reviews uploads

For early-stage research ideas, consider using short summaries rather than full drafts when possible.
