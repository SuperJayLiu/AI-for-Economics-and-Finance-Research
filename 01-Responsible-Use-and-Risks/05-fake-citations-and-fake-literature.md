# Fake Citations And Fake Literature

AI can invent literature that sounds plausible. This is especially dangerous in economics and finance because authors, working paper titles, and seminar drafts can sound credible even when false.

Never use AI-generated references without verification.

Minimum citation check:

- Does the source exist?
- Are the authors, title, year, and outlet correct?
- Does the source actually support the sentence?
- Is the paper published, working, retracted, corrected, or superseded?
- Is there a more appropriate source?

For literature reviews, ask AI to separate:

- verified sources you provided
- sources it found through search
- uncertain suggestions requiring manual checking
