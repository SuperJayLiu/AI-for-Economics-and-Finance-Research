# AI Disclosure In Papers

Disclosure expectations vary by journal, conference, institution, and funder. Check the relevant policy before submission.

A conservative disclosure practice records:

- tool or model used
- task performed
- whether AI text was used directly
- whether AI helped with code, analysis, editing, translation, or figures
- what human verification was performed

AI tools should not be listed as authors because they cannot take responsibility for the work.

When in doubt, disclose material AI assistance clearly and briefly.
