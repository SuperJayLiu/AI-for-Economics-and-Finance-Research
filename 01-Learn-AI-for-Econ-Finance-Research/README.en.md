# Learn AI for Economics and Finance Research

This is the long-form handbook. Use it when you want to understand concepts, risks, research workflows, and the reasoning behind the reusable tools elsewhere in the repo.

## What This Folder Is For

This folder explains how AI fits into economics and finance research:

- what LLMs are and are not
- how hallucinations and fake citations happen
- how to manage confidential data and coauthor work
- how to use AI for literature, theory, empirical work, coding, writing, and presentations
- how to move from simple chat to projects, skills, GitHub workflows, and agents

## How It Differs From Other Folders

- Use this folder to learn.
- Use [Ready-Made AI Research Instructions](../02-Use-Ready-Made-AI-Research-Instructions/README.en.md) when you want templates, checklists, logs, or direct instructions.

- Use [Examples and Case Studies](../04-Examples-Diagrams-and-Case-Studies/README.en.md) when you want to see workflows in action.
