# AI For Research Presentations

## What Problem This Solves

AI can help turn papers, results, and mechanisms into clear talks. The goal is not prettier slides. The goal is clearer research communication.

## When AI Helps

- talk structure
- slide sequence
- audience adaptation
- title and section framing
- figure simplification
- speaker notes
- Q&A preparation
- HTML slide generation
- converting PPT to web slides
- drafting traditional LaTeX/Beamer slides

## When AI Is Risky

- inventing results
- overstating causal claims
- simplifying theory incorrectly
- changing figure meaning
- creating flashy but distracting slides
- exposing unpublished work through public tools
- inventing answers to likely questions

## Recommended Workflow

1. Define the audience.
2. Define the talk goal.
3. Extract the core research story.
4. Build a slide outline.
5. Decide which figures and tables belong.
6. Use AI to improve visual clarity.
7. Generate draft slides.
8. Check every claim, number, label, and figure.
9. Rehearse with AI as a tough audience.
10. Export or share only after privacy review.

## What The Scholar Must Verify

- research question
- identification language
- table and figure interpretation
- economic magnitude
- standard errors and uncertainty
- policy or investment implications
- whether animation or visual design distracts from evidence
