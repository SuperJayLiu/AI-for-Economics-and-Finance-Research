# AI Use With Coauthors And Research Teams

## What Problem This Solves

AI use in joint work can create consent, disclosure, privacy, and accountability problems if teams do not agree on norms.

## Team Questions To Settle Early

- Can coauthored drafts be uploaded to AI tools?
- Which tools are allowed?
- Which files should never be uploaded?
- Can AI edit shared Overleaf text?
- Who verifies AI-generated code?
- How should AI use be logged?
- When should coauthors be told AI was used?

## Recommended Team Norm

For each project, maintain a short AI-use policy in the project README or `AGENTS.md`. Include allowed tools, forbidden materials, verification duties, and disclosure expectations.

## What The Scholar Must Verify

- coauthor consent
- data-use agreements
- institutional policy
- journal or conference policy
- accuracy of AI-assisted changes

## Sources and Workflow Influences

Draws on coauthoring norms, data privacy rules, and AI-assisted research accountability practices.
