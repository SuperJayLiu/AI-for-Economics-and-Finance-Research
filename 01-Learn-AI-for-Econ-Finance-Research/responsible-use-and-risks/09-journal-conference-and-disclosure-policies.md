# Journal Conference And Disclosure Policies

## What Problem This Solves

AI disclosure rules differ across journals, conferences, universities, and funders. They also change over time.

## Core Rules

- Policies differ.
- Policies change.
- Always check the current policy before submission.
- AI cannot be an author.
- Humans remain responsible.
- Disclose when required.

## Applies To

- journal submissions
- SSRN and working papers
- NBER or CEPR-style working paper circulation
- conferences and workshops
- referee reports
- grant proposals
- teaching material

## What To Record

- tool/model used
- task performed
- files or materials processed
- output accepted
- human verification performed
- whether disclosure is required

## Sources and Workflow Influences

Draws on publication ethics guidance, journal AI policies, and responsible research documentation practices.
