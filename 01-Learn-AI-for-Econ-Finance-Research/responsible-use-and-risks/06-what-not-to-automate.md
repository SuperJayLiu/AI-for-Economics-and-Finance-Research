# What Not To Automate

## What Problem This Solves

AI can automate labor, but not scholarly responsibility. Some decisions must remain human decisions.

## Do Not Fully Automate

- research question choice
- identification strategy
- causal interpretation
- final literature claims
- referee judgment
- confidential manuscript review
- policy or investment recommendations
- data access decisions
- final paper submission

## Recommended Use

Use AI to clarify options, list risks, propose checks, and draft materials after the researcher has made the key scholarly decision.

## What The Scholar Must Verify

- whether the task is appropriate for AI assistance
- whether policy permits using AI
- whether all claims are supported
- whether the final decision is owned by a human researcher

## Sources and Workflow Influences

Draws on responsible AI publication guidance, peer-review confidentiality norms, and economics/finance research integrity concerns.
