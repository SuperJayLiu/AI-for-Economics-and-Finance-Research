# Building Repeatable Workflows

A repeatable AI workflow is a research routine you can use again with consistent inputs, steps, outputs, and checks.

Example workflow:

- Input: paper PDF and your research question
- Rules: no invented citations; separate facts from interpretation
- Output: structured paper note
- Verification: check abstract, tables, identification section, and references
- Log: record AI output used and manual corrections

Repeatability matters because it makes AI assistance easier to audit.
