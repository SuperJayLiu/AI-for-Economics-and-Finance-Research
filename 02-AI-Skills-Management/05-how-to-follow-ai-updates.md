# How To Follow AI Updates

Follow updates at three levels:

- official product sources for feature changes
- academic and technical sources for reliability and limitations
- field-specific examples for economics and finance relevance

Avoid relying only on viral tool lists. Many are written for productivity marketing, not research quality.

Good update habits:

- check official release notes before relying on a feature
- test new tools on low-risk tasks
- record what changed in your workflow
- ignore tools that do not solve your actual research problems
