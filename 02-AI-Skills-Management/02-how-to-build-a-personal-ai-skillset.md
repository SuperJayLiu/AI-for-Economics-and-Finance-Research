# How To Build A Personal AI Skillset

Build your AI skillset around recurring research tasks.

1. List your repeated tasks: literature notes, coding, tables, writing, teaching, slides, referee responses.
2. Pick one task where errors are easy to detect.
3. Design a repeatable workflow with inputs, rules, outputs, and verification.
4. Save the workflow in a research log.
5. Only then expand to higher-risk tasks.

Do not start with the most sensitive or consequential task.
