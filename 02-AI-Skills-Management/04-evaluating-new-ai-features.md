# Evaluating New AI Features

New AI features should be evaluated through small research tasks before adoption.

Feature checklist:

- What capability does this add?
- What information does the tool need?
- Where is the information stored?
- Can outputs be cited, logged, or reproduced?
- What mistakes would be hard to detect?
- What policy or license constraints apply?
- Does it help with economics or finance research specifically?

Prefer features that improve verification, traceability, and source use.
