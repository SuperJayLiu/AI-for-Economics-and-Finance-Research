# How To Judge Online AI Advice

Ask these questions before following AI advice online:

- Who is giving the advice?
- Is it for research, business, coding, or general productivity?
- Does it apply to economics or finance?
- Is there a real example?
- Does it protect privacy and reproducibility?
- Does it require paid tools?
- Does it increase or reduce verification burden?
- Is it still current?
- Can I test it on a small task first?

Be skeptical of advice that promises to replace reading, identification work, coding checks, or expert review.
