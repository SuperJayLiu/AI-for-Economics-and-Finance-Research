# Builder-Oriented AI Learning

## Principle

Follow people and projects that build useful workflows, not sources that only repeat AI news.

## What To Extract

- repeatable workflows
- setup patterns
- safety checks
- tool evaluation methods
- examples that can be tested
- failure modes

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- tool rankings without dates
- claims that do not apply to research
- workflows that ignore privacy
- advice that discourages verification
- copied skills without license review

## Sources and Workflow Influences

Draws on builder-oriented AI learning resources, academic AI workflow guides, and official documentation.
