# Agents And Automation

AI agents can take multi-step actions such as searching, editing files, running code, or generating summaries across many documents.

They increase productivity risk as well as productivity.

Common risks:

- changing files without understanding the research design
- hiding errors behind polished summaries
- mixing raw and cleaned data
- making many small undocumented edits
- producing outputs that are hard to reproduce

Use agents only with:

- version control
- small test tasks
- clear permissions
- human approval checkpoints
- logs of what changed
