# Local Vs Cloud Models

Cloud AI tools are convenient and often stronger. Local models can reduce some privacy and control risks, but they require technical setup and may be less capable.

Use local or institution-approved systems when working with:

- restricted administrative data
- proprietary firm data
- confidential referee materials
- sensitive student data
- unpublished material subject to strict sharing limits

Local models do not solve hallucination, bias, or reasoning errors. They mainly change the privacy and control profile.
